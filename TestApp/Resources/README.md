# TestApp Resources

## `web2a.txt`

`web2a.txt` is the FreeBSD `share/dict/web2a` supplemental word list used by
the Words demo to exercise very large, realistic scroll views.

Source:
https://web.mit.edu/freebsd/head/share/dict/web2a

FreeBSD's accompanying `share/dict/README` describes `web2` as Webster's Second
International and notes that the supplemental `web2a` list contains hyphenated
terms plus noun and adverbial phrases:

https://web.mit.edu/freebsd/head/share/dict/README
